Personnel Directory

All personnel referenced across the Operations, Sales, and Accounting workflow tabs. Click a card to see the person's role, responsibilities, and which workflow tabs mention them. Names highlighted in purple in Inventory, Sales, and Accounting pages link directly here.

How cross-links work: When a person's name is highlighted and underlined in an Operations, Sales, or Accounting page, clicking it will switch to this tab and scroll to that entry. To add or remove a name from the link list, edit cross_reference_terms.txt then run python workflow_generate.py.
Field & Delivery Staff
Driver Personnel Field — Delivery INV ▾
Role
Delivery driver responsible for transporting cylinders from the warehouse to client sites and returning empty cylinders to the plant.
Key Responsibilities
Receives DR from Warehouse · Drives to client locations with Pahinante · Collects client-signed DR · Weighs empty cylinders on return · Submits signed DR and AR to Accounting
Pahinante Personnel Field — Delivery Helper INV ▾
Role
Delivery helper who accompanies the Driver on all delivery runs. Physically handles the cylinders at the client site and performs weight recording.
Key Responsibilities
Weighs cylinders on-site at client location · Records cylinder measurements · Delivers cylinders to client · Collects empty cylinders for return

Key Responsibilities
Reads Sales Order · Schedules delivery vehicle · Assigns driver and load capacity · Plans delivery route · Coordinates pickup schedules
