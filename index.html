Multi-Page SOP Portal

The live site opens as a tabbed SOP workspace. Each of the Main, Operations, Sales, and Accounting tabs is organized as an SOP Manual, Operational Guidelines, Approval Matrix & Controls, and Current vs. Future Workflow per stage. The Subprocess, Forms, and Personnel tabs are reference indexes — terms highlighted in Operations, Sales, and Accounting link directly back to entries in these tabs.
