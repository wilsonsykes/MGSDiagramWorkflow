1SOP Manual — Step-by-Step Procedure

  1. Each department page (Operations, Sales, Accounting) is organized in the same four-part order: SOP Manual, Operational Guidelines, Approval Matrix & Controls, and Current vs. Future Workflow.
  2. Start with the SOP Manual - the numbered, step-by-step procedure for how work actually gets done today.
  3. Read the Operational Guidelines next - the policy rules that govern how those steps are applied and where staff discretion ends.
  4. Check the Approval Matrix & Controls - who is authorized to initiate, review, and approve each transaction, and what control activity is in place.
  5. Finish with Current vs. Future Workflow - today's manual/paper reality next to the target automated state.
  6. A green "Confirmed with source data" badge means the stage is grounded in real, Masagana-specific source data; an amber "Partially confirmed" badge means part of that stage is still a placeholder, with the gap called out explicitly in a gap note.
  7. Cross-linked terms (personnel names, forms, subprocesses) are underlined - click one to jump to its entry in the Personnel, Forms, or Subprocess tab.

2Operational Guidelines

  • Financial Controls & Credit Policy: no invoice or plant release proceeds on an account with unresolved past dues unless a supervisor has explicitly approved a passable balance.
  • Weighing, Residue & Inventory Integrity: gains, losses, and commission are computed only from a matched pair of weigh-in and weigh-out readings; an unmatched reading is held for investigation, never estimated.
  • Approval & Escalation Discipline: any transaction blocked or disputed at its normal approval level escalates through the standard chain rather than being resolved informally between the two parties involved.
  • Documentation & Reconciliation: the person who initiates a transaction is not the same person who posts it to the GL or reconciles it - segregation of duties applies at every posting step.

4Current vs. Future Workflow

Current — Traditional / Manual
  • Process knowledge for Operations, Sales, and Accounting has lived across separate documents, transcripts, and institutional memory, plus different tools (Google Sheets, ACCPAC, AppSheet, Viber).
Future — Target / Automated
  • One SOP portal - GitHub-hosted, JSON-driven, and cross-linked to Personnel/Forms/Subprocess - as the single source of truth for how MGC actually runs, with a clear owner for each transaction.
