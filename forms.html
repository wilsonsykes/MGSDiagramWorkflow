Forms & Documents Index

All forms and documents referenced across the Operations, Sales, and Accounting workflow tabs. Click a card to read the description, purpose, and which workflow tabs use that document. Terms highlighted in green in Inventory, Sales, and Accounting pages link directly here.

How cross-links work: When a form or document name is highlighted and underlined in an Operations, Sales, or Accounting page, clicking it will switch to this tab and scroll to that entry. To add or remove a form from the link list, edit cross_reference_terms.txt then run python workflow_generate.py.
Delivery Receipt (DR) Form OPSACG ▾
Description
The primary delivery document that accompanies each cylinder delivery. The Driver carries the DR for multiple clients. The client signs the DR on receipt. The signed DR is submitted to Accounting for General Ledger reconciliation.
Usage
Warehouse generates 2 DRs and 2 CRs per delivery · Driver carries DR to client · Client signs · Driver submits signed DR to Acctg · Acctg reconciles GL with signed DR and AR
Sales Order Form Form OPSSAL ▾
Description
Created by the Sales Rep for dealer and retail client orders. Includes the delivery or pickup date. Used by the Delivery Head to schedule routes. Signed by the client if pickup with terms.
Usage
Sales Rep creates Sales Order Form · Specifies delivery/pickup date · Delivery Head reads to schedule route · Client signs for pickup with terms
Picklist Form OPS ▾
Description
Warehouse picking document generated per order. Lists the specific cylinders (type, size, quantity) to be picked from storage for a delivery. Generated before the Delivery Receipt.
Usage
Warehouse generates Picklist after order confirmation · Staff picks cylinders per Picklist · Feeds into DR generation
Credit Notes (CR) Form OPS ▾
Description
Document issued to the client recording the cylinder weight residue credits. Contains the cylinder serial number and weight residue. Warehouse generates 2 DRs and 2 CRs per delivery. Threshold: 1–3 kg residue triggers roundup/rounddown classification.
Usage
Pahinante records weight · Driver provides Credit Notes to client with cylinder serial # and weight residue · Submitted to Accounting
General Ledger (GL) Form ▾
Description
The central accounting ledger entry updated for each delivery and financial transaction. Acctg Staff reconciles the General Ledger with the signed DR and AR after each delivery cycle.
Usage
Warehouse submits DR to Accounting to make General Ledger and Inventory entry · Acctg Staff reconciles GL with signed DR and AR
Receiving Report (RR) Form OPS ▾
Description
Document filled by the Plant Manager when a Lorry Truck delivery arrives from the Refinery Supplier. Records the volume unloaded to the Storage Tank. Any changes in quantity after the RR are reported separately.
Usage
Plant Manager fills RR on truck arrival · Documents volume unloaded · Post-RR changes reported separately · Feeds into inventory records
Purchase Order (PO) Form OPS ▾
Description
Procurement document forwarded by the Purchaser to the Refinery Supplier to order gas stock. Triggers the inbound delivery process at the plant, which generates the Receiving Report.
Usage
Purchaser forwards PO to Refinery Supplier · Supplier schedules Lorry Truck delivery · Plant Manager receives and logs in RR
Invoice Form OPSACG ▾
Description
Billing document issued to clients. Collected by Accounting together with the DR and Sales Order as a package for receivables reconciliation. Linked to the Accounts Receivable subprocess.
Usage
Issued on delivery · Accounting collects DR + Sales Order + Invoice · Used for monthly receivables summary and bank reconciliation
Sales Performance Report (SPR) Form SAL ▾
Description
Bi-weekly or monthly sales report printed by IT Head depending on inventory volume. Fields: sales per kg, total sales, net sales, cost of sales (with formula), Gross Profit Margin per kg and total GPM.
Usage
IT Head prints SPR · Shared with management · Informs Pricing Matrix decisions
Loading/Unloading Truck Forms (LUT) Form ▾
Description
Cover cylinder sizes 2.7 kg, 11 kg, and 50 kg. Used to balance empty and field cylinder data when preparing the Incentive template for payroll.
Usage
Accounting uses LUT forms (check 2.7kg, 11kg, 50kg) to balance empty and field cylinders · Feeds into Incentive template · Used for Commercial and Retail payroll
Incentive Template Form ACG ▾
Description
Template used for bi-monthly (15th and 30th) payroll incentive computation for Commercial and Retail channels. Populated using LUT forms and cylinder balance data.
Usage
Prepare Incentive template for Commercial and Retail · Use LUT forms to balance cylinder data · Encode in payroll on 15th and 30th
Aged Receivables Form ACG ▾
Description
Summary report of outstanding client receivables organized by age and duration. Printed monthly as part of the Accounts Receivable subprocess to identify overdue accounts.
Usage
Print summary of Aged Receivables monthly · Review overdue accounts · Escalate for dispute resolution or write-off
Payroll Summary Form ACG ▾
Description
Monthly payroll summary document for all staff. Consolidates all incentive computations and salary data. Used as the official payroll record for the period.
Usage
Prepared after Incentive template is finalized · Covers all staff · Released on the 15th and 30th of each month
Budget PNL Form ACG ▾
Description
Budgeted Profit and Loss report for industrial projects. Sets the expected financial targets against which the Actual PNL is compared during project review.
Usage
Prepared at project start · Compared against Actual PNL during review · Part of Industrial Projects tracking subprocess
Actual PNL Form ACG ▾
Description
Actual Profit and Loss performance report for industrial projects. Compared against the Budget PNL to measure project financial performance.
Usage
Updated as project progresses · Compared with Budget PNL · Part of Industrial Projects tracking subprocess
MGC LPG Pricing Matrix Form SAL ▾
Description
Dashboard document for deciding gas prices across all sales avenues. Reviewed 1–3 times per month in a General Meeting. Covers Sales Price, MPL, Total Cost (Acq Cost + Hauling), kg per avenue, and volume discounts for different radii.
Avenues
Commercial to Retail and semi-dealers · MPL volume discounts by radius · End-user SRP vs. competitors
Promissory Note Form ACG ▾
Description
Required fallback payment-security document for a dealer who cannot issue Post-Dated Checks. Executed before installment credit is extended in place of PDCs.
Usage
Used when dealer cannot provide PDCs · Executed before installment terms are granted · Filed alongside the dealer's AR record
Post-Dated Checks (PDC) Form ACG ▾
Description
Default payment security collected from dealers on installment terms (about 70% of dealers). A Promissory Note is the required fallback when a dealer cannot issue PDCs.
Usage
Collected for installment orders · Tracked in Google Sheets by due date · Missing PDC triggers Promissory Note requirement
Entry/Exit Slip (EES) Form ▾
Description
Gate document issued to a dealer or retail vehicle on plant entry, recording cylinder quantities and tank classification. Validated by the Guard on both entry and final exit.
Usage
Guard issues on vehicle entry · Guard validates tank classification · Guard performs final exit verification before release
General Refilling Report (GRR) Form ▾
Description
generated from the Checker's AppSheet entry after a Dealer plant refill. AppSheet quantities must match the official receipt before release, and Accounting verifies it against that receipt.
Usage
Checker encodes transaction in AppSheet · Basis of the GRR · Accounting verifies AppSheet vs. official receipt before release
