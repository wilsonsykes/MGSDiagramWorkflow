Subprocess Index

All operational subprocesses referenced across the Inventory, Sales, and Accounting workflow tabs. Click a card to read the description and see which workflow tabs reference that subprocess. Terms highlighted in blue in Inventory, Sales, and Accounting pages link directly here.

How cross-links work: When a subprocess name is highlighted and underlined in an Inventory, Sales, or Accounting page, clicking it will switch to this tab and scroll to that entry. To add or remove a subprocess from the link list, edit cross_reference_terms.txt then run python workflow_generate.py.
Process Order Subprocess INVSALACG ▾
Description
Order intake, scheduling, and validation workflow. Begins when a client order arrives through any sales channel and ends when the order is confirmed and queued for fulfilment.
Key Steps
Receive client order · Validate cylinder type and quantity · Schedule delivery or pickup · Assign reference number
Fulfil Order Subprocess INVSAL ▾
Description
Order execution and physical delivery to the client. Covers warehouse picking, driver dispatch, on-site delivery or pickup, and client signature collection.
Key Steps
Generate Picklist · Pick cylinders · Dispatch Driver and Pahinante · Deliver/pickup · Collect signed DR · Return empty cylinders
Invoicing Subprocess INVACG ▾
Description
Billing and invoice issuance process. Covers generation of the Invoice document, pairing with the Delivery Receipt and Sales Order, and submission to Accounting for reconciliation.
Key Steps
Generate Invoice · Attach to DR and Sales Order · Submit to Accounting · Record in General Ledger
Compute Commercial Gains Subprocess INV ▾
Description
Commercial sector commission and profit computation. Gathers weigh-in and weigh-out records at both the client site and plant, then records and distributes commissions.
Key Steps
Gather weight records · Record in Gsheet and ACPAC · Compute commission · Distribute commission
Compute Operational Gains Subprocess INV ▾
Description
Operational commission tracking and distribution for dealer channel orders. Includes cylinder weight reconciliation and sales commission calculations.
Key Steps
Track delivery weights · Calculate per-kg commissions · Distribute to Sales Rep
Compute Gains Subprocess INVSAL ▾
Description
General commission and gains computation covering all sales channels. Used as a summary step for reconciling total gains per delivery cycle.
Key Steps
Aggregate delivery records · Apply commission formula · Generate gains summary
Commission Distribution Subprocess INVSAL ▾
Description
Calculation and distribution of commissions to field staff and sales personnel. Follows the gains computation step and precedes payroll encoding.
Key Steps
Calculate per-person commission · Validate totals · Encode in payroll · Distribute
Stock Transfer Subprocess INV ▾
Description
Gas content refilling from the Storage Tank to individual cylinders following the FIFO (First-In-First-Out) process. Managed by Plant Staff using the gauge measurement system.
Key Steps
Measure tank volume · Refill cylinders FIFO · Document fill quantities · Update inventory records
FIFO Subprocess INV ▾
Description
First-In-First-Out inventory management protocol applied during cylinder refilling. Ensures oldest gas stock is used first to maintain quality and accurate costing.
Key Steps
Identify oldest stock (Tank 1 vs Tank 2) · Refill in FIFO order · Track residue type (Regular vs WIWO)
Pull-out Calendar Subprocess INV ▾
Description
Scheduled cylinder retrieval dates at client sites. Coordinates the pull-out of empty or overdue cylinders based on an agreed calendar to maintain cylinder inventory balance.
Key Steps
Set pull-out schedule with client · Assign Driver and Pahinante · Retrieve and weigh cylinders · Update inventory
Sales Reports Subprocess SAL ▾
Description
Bi-weekly or monthly Sales Performance Report (SPR) generation. IT Head prints the report based on inventory volume; covers sales per kg, total sales, net sales, cost of sales, and Gross Profit Margin.
Key Steps
IT Head prints SPR · Review sales per kg and total sales · Compute net sales and GPM · Distribute to management
Pricing Matrix Subprocess SAL ▾
Description
LPG price-setting process using the MGC LPG Pricing Matrix dashboard. Held 1–3 times per month in a General Meeting; covers Sales Price, MPL, Total Cost (Acq Cost + Hauling), and kg per avenue.
Key Steps
General Meeting · Review acquisition and hauling costs · Set price per avenue · Update Pricing Matrix dashboard
Price Cascade Process Subprocess SAL ▾
Description
Pricing change communication and rollout to all relevant contacts and channels. Mel emails the updates; Jabert logs audit records; each staff member cascades to their assigned client/channel.
Contacts
Mel → email update · Jabert → audit log · Jobert → contact centers/marketing · Mel & Lou → Comm/Bulk · Claire → Retail · Jen → Makati
End-User Pricing Subprocess SAL ▾
Description
SRP and competitor pricing comparison process. Mel and Joebert regularly update competitor prices; the result informs End-User SRP decisions in the Pricing Matrix dashboard.
Key Steps
Mel and Joebert gather competitor prices · Update comparison dashboard · Feed into Pricing Matrix meeting
Accounts Receivable Subprocess ACG ▾
Description
Collection and reconciliation of outstanding client invoices. Includes reviewing monthly summary tables, printing Aged Receivables, bank reconciliation, and resolving customer disputes.
Key Steps
Collect DR + Sales Order + Invoice · Check monthly invoice summary · Print Aged Receivables · Bank reconciliation · Resolve disputes
Financial Statements Subprocess ACG ▾
Description
Monthly reporting cycle for Profit & Loss Statement, Cash Flow Statement, and Balance Sheet. Each requires all prior entries to be encoded, then tables refreshed and statements printed.
Statements
Profit & Loss (PNL) · Cash Flow · Balance Sheet — all refreshed and printed monthly
Payroll Subprocess ACG ▾
Description
Bi-monthly (15th and 30th) payroll and incentive computation. Uses the Incentive template and LUT forms to balance empty and field cylinder data for Commercial and Retail channels.
Key Steps
Prepare Incentive template · Apply LUT forms · Validate cylinder counts · Encode in payroll system · Release on 15th & 30th
Industrial Projects Subprocess ACG ▾
Description
Industrial project budget vs. actual PNL and timeline tracking. Produces Budget PNL, Actual PNL, and timeline documents for each industrial engagement.
Outputs
Budget PNL · Actual PNL · Timeline document
Bank Reconciliation Subprocess ACG ▾
Description
Matching bank statement records with internal general ledger amounts. Part of the Accounts Receivable workflow; resolved discrepancies are flagged as customer disputes or write-offs.
Key Steps
Print bank summary · Match against GL · Identify variances · Resolve disputes or write-off
