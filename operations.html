✎CURRENT — TRADITIONALMANUAL
✕Process Order: C1.1.1 Client orders 22kg and 50kg cylinder variants.
✕Fulfil Order: C1.2.1 Using Sales Order, Delivery Head schedules delivery [vehicle, capacity, route].
✕C1.1.2 Warehouse generates Picklist and picks LPG/products.
✕C1.1.3 Warehouse generates Delivery Receipt (2 DRs and 2 CRs).
✕C1.1.4.1 Warehouse submits DR to Acctg to make General Ledger and Inventory.
✕C1.1.4.2 Driver gets Delivery Receipt (DR) from Warehouse for multiple clients.
✕Driver and Pahinante drive to client locations.
✕C1.1.4.3 No Delivery/For Pick up. Warehouse gives DR to customer for signature.
✕C1.1.5.1 On client site, Pahinante weighs and records the cylinders, then delivers them.
✕C1.1.5.2 Client signs DR and picks up items and returns empty cylinder.
✕C1.1.5.3 Check threshold 1-3kg, above and below, roundup/down, classification.
✕C1.1.6 Provide Credit Notes to client with cylinder serial number and weight residue.
✕C1.1.7 Driver returns to Plant and weighs empty cylinders.
✕C1.1.8 Driver submits record and signed DR and AR to Acctg.
✕C1.1.9 Acctg Staff reconciles General Ledger with signed DR and AR.
✕Compute Commercial Gains: Gather weight in and weigh out records at Client and Plant.
✕Record in Gsheet and ACPAC.
✕Compute Commission and Distribute.
